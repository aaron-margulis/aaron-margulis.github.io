About Me

Originally from Chicago, IL, I am currently studying Economics with a minor in Computer Science at Washington University in St. Louis, expecting to graduate in May, 2022. I have a passion for sports analytics, an interest in capital markets, and I love games of strategy. I won the 2019 Society for American Baseball Research Diamond Dollars Case Competition, I have conducted an extensive research project on 4th downs in the NFL, along with other projects, for the WashU Sports Analytics Club which I help run, and last year I worked on an expected-goals-based soccer research tool.
