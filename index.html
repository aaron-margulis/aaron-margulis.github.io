About Me

Originally from Chicago, Illinois, I am currently studying Economics with a minor in Computer Science at Washington University in St. Louis, expecting to graduate in May, 2022. I have a love for sports and a passion for sports analytics. Amongst other things, I won the 2019 Society for American Baseball Research Diamond Dollars Case Competition, I've done an extensive research project on 4th downs in the NFL, and I am currently working on an expected-goals-based soccer research tool. I am also curious about financial markets, and have a general interest in generating predictions through the use of data.
